Statement of Objectives

The primary objective of this workshop is to present the latest advancements in human-AI/autonomy interaction and integration, exploring how control-theoretic principles can provide a rigorous foundation for AI-driven autonomous systems.

We seek to bridge the gap between data-driven AI and formal control, emphasizing a strategic equilibrium between learning-based adaptation and model-based verification.

Call for Participation & Posters

We invite two-page extended abstracts highlighting work-in-progress or recently published results. Accepted abstracts will be given a poster presentation during the coffee breaks.

  • Human-AI interaction and integration
  • Cyber-physical human systems
  • Trust calibration in autonomous systems
  • Shared autonomy and joint decision-making
